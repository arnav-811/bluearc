Who We Are

Precision, strategy, and innovation — the foundation of everything we do.

BlueArc is a financial and business advisory firm headquartered in Mumbai. Our team brings together highly qualified Chartered Accountants, legal advisors, and business strategists with deep expertise across taxation, compliance, business structuring, outsourced accounting, and India market entry.

We are results-oriented and client-first. Every engagement starts with understanding your business — how it operates, where it's going, and what stands in the way. From there, we build the systems, strategies, and compliance frameworks to help you grow without friction.

We've been doing this since 2009. Whether you're a startup at day one or a multinational entering a new market, you'll get the same standard: precise, ethical, and genuinely useful advisory.
