Business Setup Manufacturing

UK Manufacturer Sets Up India Subsidiary — Zero Compliance Gaps in Year One

A UK-based precision engineering company engaged BlueArc to manage the end-to-end setup of its Indian subsidiary, navigate the regulatory landscape, and ensure full compliance from day one of operations.

90
Days to full operational setup
₹0
Penalties in Year 1
100%
Regulatory filings on-time

The Challenge

The client had no prior India presence and needed to incorporate a private limited company, obtain all registrations (GST, PF, ESI, import/export code), hire local staff, and start invoicing Indian customers — all within three months of board approval.

Our Approach

BlueArc acted as the single point of contact coordinating company incorporation, tax registrations, banking setup, and payroll. We provided a dedicated Virtual CFO to manage the finance function during the first year, freeing the leadership team to focus on sales.

Indirect Taxation Real Estate

Real Estate Developer Recovers ₹1.2 Cr in GST Input Tax Credits

A mid-size real estate developer had been missing substantial Input Tax Credit (ITC) claims due to incorrect GST classification of construction expenses. BlueArc conducted a full GST audit and restructured their accounting to recover the unclaimed credits.

₹1.2Cr
ITC recovered
3 yrs
Historical data reviewed
40%
Reduction in GST liability going forward

The Challenge

The client's accounting team had been using a blanket blocked-credit classification on construction inputs, resulting in significant GST overpayments. They also faced a GST notice for mismatch between GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B over two financial years.

Our Approach

Our team performed a transaction-level GST review, reclassified eligible input credits, filed amended returns where possible, and provided a detailed representation to the GST department resolving the mismatch notice without any penalty.

Client Outcome

"We had no idea we were leaving so much money on the table. BlueArc's audit was thorough, and the team managed the GST department notice professionally. A very tangible financial win for us."

— CFO, Mumbai-based Real Estate Developer

Startup India Software & IT

SaaS Startup Secures DPIIT Recognition and ₹80L in Tax Exemptions

A Bangalore-based SaaS company approached BlueArc for Startup India registration (DPIIT recognition), Angel Tax exemption under Section 56(2)(viib), and income tax exemption under Section 80-IAC. BlueArc managed the full filing and secured all three within four months.

₹80L
Tax exemptions secured
4 mo
End-to-end timeline
3
Government recognitions obtained

The Challenge

The startup had recently raised a seed round and was concerned about Angel Tax liability. They also needed DPIIT recognition for their investor to complete SEBI filings. The founders had no prior experience with government compliance processes.

Our Approach

BlueArc prepared the DPIIT application, obtained CBDT tax exemption under 80-IAC, and filed the Form-2 for Angel Tax exemption. We also structured their cap table and set up a compliant ESOP plan during the same engagement.

Client Outcome

"BlueArc took ownership of the entire Startup India process. We didn't have to navigate a single government portal ourselves. The tax savings gave us critical runway in our early stage."

— Co-Founder, Bangalore SaaS Startup
